CHEMICAL PRODUCTS

CHARACTERISTIC:

Mega Mix -18 is a latent based formula for mixing in Melamine Resin in Impregnation line with a major advantage of replacing all additives. It is a special composition and assures a very good reactivity during the Resin Polymerization and excellent impregnation bath stability for temperature up to 35C.

MODE OF USE:

Mega Mix - 18 can be used with all types of MF Resin for Impregnation of Paper either short cycle or HPL. We suggest to mix the Mega Mix-18 within Resin solution in order to get uniform solution. The required dose for liquid Resin is 0.4 to 0.5%, but it is always better to test in advance (Lab Scale) in a way to find the best dosage to reach the required Gel Time. The Gel Time may easily be further reduced by the addition of our product Hardener MF-1122, if required, the required dosages 0.35 to 0.40%

How to make one ton batch of MF Resin:
1. MF Resin. 1000 kg.
2. MF Hardener 1122. 3.5 to 4 kg. (as per required gel time)
3. Mega Mix – 18. 5 Kg.

Special Instruction:

Mix above additives properly just for 5 To 7 Minutes.

STORAGE:

The product reacts freezes in reversible ways below 5C: We suggest to store the product 15 to 40C to guarantee an optimum shelf life that is six months in closed and original packing.

PACKING:

Product Name:

Hardener MF – 1122

End Use:

Water based Melamine Formaldehyde Resins for Impregnation of Decorative Papers Hardener MF-1122 is used in water-based melamine formaldehyde and other resins, it has been developed for HPL and short cycling decorative papers.

Properties:

Physical state : Liquid
Density : Approx. 1.2 kg / L
pH : Approx. 6 – 8
Flash point : non-flammable
Shelf life : 1 year

Suggested Dose:

0.3 – 0.4% depending upon the required gel time and shelf of resin the ideal dosage level to achieve the short test hardening cycle has to be evaluated by the customer.

Storage and Handling:

No special measures are required It is proved to be good for use for 1 year under room temperature. At low temperature, the product may settle, hence, a proper stirring is required to be done to restore its properties. The product can be safely stored in HDPE Drums / cans or Stainless-Steel Barrels.

Packing:

Standard packing type is 30 / 35 kg Plastic Jerry Cans (used – Standard quality)

Product Name:

Hardener UF–2233

End Use:

UF Resin for impregnation of Decorative Papers Hardener UF-2453 is clear yellowish liquid which cures urea formaldehyde resin satisfactorily on decorative papers for lamination. It accelerates curing with prolonged resin pot life.

Properties:

Physical state : Clear & yellowish liquid
pH : 8 – 10
Density : 1.2
Flash point : non flammable
Shelf life : 1 year

Suggested Dose:

Recommended dose is 0.4% depending upon the required curing time. Pressing conditions, drying, resinification and type of paper are also to be considered.

Storage and Handling

It is proved to be good for use for 1 year under room temperature. At low temperature the product may settle, hence, a proper stirring is required to be done to restore its properties. The product can be safely stored in HDPE Drums / cans or Stainless Steel Barrels.

First Aid Measures

No special measures required. Our experience shows that the product has no harmful effects on human body, however, in any case eyes contact should be avoided.
